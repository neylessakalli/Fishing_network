# Image Setup Instructions

## Banner Image

The main banner image (`mordu-banner.jpg`) should be placed in the `public/` folder.

**Required file:**
- `public/mordu-banner.jpg` - The main banner image with "Mordu de la Pêche" text

The banner image should be:
- A wide horizontal banner (recommended: 1920x400px or similar aspect ratio)
- Features the "Mordu de la Pêche" branding
- Dark brown/orange/black color scheme matching the website aesthetic

## Logo Image

The header logo image (`mordu-logo.jpg`) should be placed in the `public/` folder.

**Required file:**
- `public/mordu-logo.jpg` - A square or rectangular logo image (recommended: 200x200px or 400x100px)
- If not available, the site will fallback to the existing logo.svg

## Additional Photos

The website includes a photo gallery section that displays fishing adventure photos. Place the following images in the `public/` folder:

**Required files:**
- `public/mordu-photo-1.jpg` - Fishing expedition photo
- `public/mordu-photo-2.jpg` - Big catch photo
- `public/mordu-photo-3.jpg` - Water adventure photo
- `public/mordu-photo-4.jpg` - Exotic destination photo

**Photo recommendations:**
- High quality images (minimum 800x450px, recommended 1920x1080px)
- Related to "Mordu de la Pêche" fishing adventures
- Match the dark brown/orange/black aesthetic
- Show fishing scenes, catches, or adventure moments

## Where to Find Images

You can find "Mordu de la Pêche" related images from:
- Official "Mordu de la Pêche" YouTube channel thumbnails
- Official website or social media accounts
- Fishing adventure stock photos that match the aesthetic
- Screenshots from episodes (with proper permissions)

## Note

If images are not available, the website will still function but may show broken image placeholders. The banner image is the most important as it appears in the header and hero section.
